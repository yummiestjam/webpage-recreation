Privacy Policy

Your Information

Summary: We store the information you provide to Tatem, but we do not store your payment information directly. We also automatically collect information as you use our platform. You can access, update or delete information in your account.

This is information that you provide to us through text fields, such as your name, email address, and payment information. The information we collect differs depending on which subscription tier you select, but you may have to provide a combination of your email address, first and last name, and payment information. You may also sign up using a Google account. We will ask permission to access basic information from your Google account, such as your name and email address. You can choose to stop sharing that information with us at any time by going to Google and removing Tatem’s access to that account and your ability to sign in.

You also have the option to add additional information to your Tatem account, which we store along with any actions that you make on Tatem. By using Tatem, you agree that your personal information that you provide directly to us, or that we collect through your use of our products and services, may be transferred to and stored in the United States and handled as described in this Policy. Tatem does not store your credit card information directly.

We also collect information automatically as you navigate our website and products or through our third party analytics providers. We may store usage information such as the type of device you use to access Tatem, your operating system, browser type, IP address, and device ID, the pages you visit or request, links clicked, referring sites, user interactions, and your search terms. We also derive your location from your self-disclosed country, your IP address, and from your credit card.

You can access and update certain information associated with your account within your account. Additionally, you can permanently delete your account at any time by emailing us at [email protected] and requesting account deletion. Please be aware that this is a final act and cannot be reversed.

Use of Cookies

Summary: We use cookies to understand how you use Tatem, provide our service, and provide experiences that are tailored to you. You can manage cookies within your browser, but if you reject or delete them Tatem may not function as intended.

We use Cookies on our website that collect information to allow us and third parties to understand your use of Tatem. A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. This small text file enables a site to remember your actions and preferences for a specified period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter your sign in or settings information each time you visit a new page on our site.

Not all cookies are strictly necessary for the website to work, but it will provide you with a better user experience. If you choose to delete or block these cookies, some features of this site may not work as intended. Cookies are not used to identify you personally and are not used for any purpose other than those described here in our Privacy Policy.

Some cookie and similar technology functions are necessary and vital to ensuring that Tatem works properly for visitors and members, such as maintaining the security, safety, and integrity on Tatem, authentication and signing in (including remembering permissions and consents you have granted), and ensuring the ability to securely complete transactions.

Cookies enable your personalized views and settings. Some cookies help us provide localized experiences — for example, by making sure you see Tatem in your current time zone.

You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. If you reject or delete cookies, our Services may no longer function as intended. Each browser provides different mechanisms for managing cookies. Look at your browser’s help menu to determine the best way to modify your browser’s cookie storage.

Performance Data

Summary: We utilize performance data to make improvements to your experience on Tatem.

Some technologies help provide performance data on how Tatem is functioning in order to make improvements to our products and services. Data collected on site functionality and speed, how Tatem is used, and detecting and gathering reporting on bugs helps improve Tatem and the services we offer.
