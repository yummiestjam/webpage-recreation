Company

The software tools we use every day are often dull, outdated, and mediocre—email is no exception. Despite being one of the most critical tools in the modern world, email hasn't meaningfully evolved in decades. It's slow, cluttered, and overwhelming, with a never-ending flood of spam and marketing emails making it harder than ever to focus on what truly matters. We believe it's time for a change.

By focusing on simplicity, speed, and intelligence, we aim to make email delightful—designed not just for today but for the future. Our vision is to help the billions of people who use email each day feel in control of their inboxes, empowered by tools that work for them. We hope you'll give Tatem a try and join us as we deliver the next generation of email.
